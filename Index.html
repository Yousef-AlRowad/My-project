What is CCP?

CCP stands for Cross-Curriculum Program. It is a learning approach that combines different subjects, like science, math, art, and history, into one project or activity. This helps students see how all subjects are connected in real life.

    Why did AIS introduce CCP?

    Al-Rowad International School (AIS) introduced CCP to make learning more engaging and meaningful. By linking subjects together, students can think creatively, solve problems better, and develop skills they can use in the real world.

    How does CCP impact students?

    CCP helps students by:Making learning fun and engaging through creative projects.
    Building important skills, like teamwork, critical thinking, and communication.
    Preparing them for the future, where solving problems often requires knowledge from different areas.
